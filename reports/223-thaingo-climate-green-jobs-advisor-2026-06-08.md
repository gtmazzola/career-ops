## C) Nivel y Estrategia

**JD level:** Technical Advisor — senior practitioner who sets direction, not just implements
**Candidate natural level for this archetype:** Programme Support / Junior Officer

The role expects someone who has already designed and delivered climate+green jobs programs at scale. Gavin's experience is deep on the analytical and documentation side (concept notes, results frameworks, reports) but light on direct program management and delivery.

**If applying (not recommended below 3.0):**
- Lead with UNIDO's SDG 9 work as evidence of analytical depth in sustainable industrialisation/green economy
- Frame the EUR 12M proposal as "green economy investment design" covering green jobs
- Cite Thailand/Lao PDR PCP work to show country context
- Acknowledge the stretch explicitly in cover letter; aim for the role as a growth opportunity
- Request a 3-month probationary review rather than waiting for annual review
