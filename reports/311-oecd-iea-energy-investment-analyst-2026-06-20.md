**Gaps:**

1. **5-7 years experience** — Hard blocker. JD specifies this as a minimum requirement. Candidate has ~2 years. Will fail CV screening unless application screener weighs models/projects heavily.

2. **Finance sector experience** — The IEA specifically quantifies investment flows, financial risks, and returns. Candidate's energy modeling is techno-economic (costs, capacity, dispatch) rather than investment/finance analytics. Meaningful gap in framing financial market data.

3. **Advanced Excel** — Not documented in CV. Addressable quickly.

**Mitigation:**
- Gap 1: Frame SMR-Solar optimization + carport LCOE as quantitative energy investment modeling. UNIDO EUR 12M IKI proposal as exposure to investment design. Strong academic credentials compensate partially for years gap.
- Gap 2: Carport investment scenario (PPA vs market rate, IRR analysis) is the best available evidence; mention LCOE, financing options, and incentive review explicitly.
- Gap 3: Easily remedied; not a first-order issue.
