## C) Nivel y Estrategia

**JD level:** Junior (2 years minimum) — technically reachable but tight
**Candidate level for this archetype:** Programme Support (project coordination, documentation)

The seniority match is reasonable but the domain mismatch is the fatal issue. SLCP is a specific technical domain — recruiters will filter for it. Without CCAC or SLCP background, application success is low.

**If applying anyway:**
- The transferable skills (multi-agency coordination, governance documentation, analytical writing) are genuine
- A strong cover letter could argue that UNIDO's SDG 9 work on industrial development intersects with methane/black carbon through industrial process emissions — a stretch but not entirely false
- Acknowledge the gap directly; show willingness to self-educate on SLCP fundamentals quickly
